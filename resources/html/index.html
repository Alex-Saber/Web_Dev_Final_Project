Welcome to ATAC arcade! ATAC was created by Alex, Trung, and Angelic in a Full Stack Web Development class taught by Caternia. Our site offers a variety of games: Flappy Bird, Snake, and Space Invaders!. You may either play without an account, or create one in order to keep track of your personal highscores. We hope you enjoy our services. Enjoy!
